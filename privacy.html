Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Stokita ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

  • Account info: Name and email via Sign in with Apple.
  • Business data: Products, orders, customers, inventory you enter into the app.
  • Images: Product photos you upload.

How We Use Data

We use your data solely to provide the app's functionality. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Data Storage

Data is stored securely on Cloudflare infrastructure. Passwords are not stored (we use Sign in with Apple).

Subscriptions

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple. We receive only the subscription status, not payment details.

Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app settings, or contact us at support@stokita.app.
